Evidence Rule

No unsupported accusations. No private victim names. No screenshots as proof. No rumor framed as fact. Every item must either link to a source or be marked clearly as not verified.

CONFIRMED = official / court / verified source DEVELOPING = credible reporting, still moving ESTIMATE = modelled or NGO estimate REPORTED = claim logged, not necessarily verified COMPENSATED = claim accepted by scheme or court MIGRATION ENCOUNTER = border/contact statistic, not unique persons ASYLUM CLAIM = application statistic, not illegal-entry count NOT VERIFIED = claim seen, not supported CAUSATION WARNING = report does not prove cause

Migration Display Rule

Do not write: “X million illegal immigrants entered” unless a reliable source specifically estimates that exact category.

Use instead: “border encounters,” “irregular crossings,” “asylum applications,” “returns/removals,” “estimated unauthorized population,” “departures,” or “net migration,” depending on the dataset.
