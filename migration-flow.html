Evidence Rule

A border encounter, asylum application, removal, return, visa-overstay estimate, unauthorized-population estimate, and illegal-entry estimate are different categories.

Official sexual-offence totals exist in national datasets. The harder question is whether the dataset contains a clean immigration-status, asylum-status, irregular-status, suspect, charge, and conviction split.

Sexual Offence Statistics / Migrant-Status Split By Country

Every listed country has official sexual-offence or sexual-violence statistics. The missing piece is not the existence of sexual-assault figures; it is the clean source split by immigration status, asylum status, irregular status, suspect stage, charge stage, and conviction stage.

OFFICIAL SEXUAL-OFFENCE STATS AVAILABLE

United Kingdom

Status: Immigration-status split not clean.

Official total sexual-offence and rape data available through ONS, Home Office police recorded crime, MoJ courts, and police force data. The missing piece is a single comparable table linking immigration status through suspect, charge, and conviction.

Available: offence totals; victim/survey lanes; police recorded crime; court/prosecution lanes.

Missing: irregular-status split; visa-overstay split; asylum-status split; clean suspect-to-conviction immigration-status bridge.

OFFICIAL SEXUAL-OFFENCE STATS AVAILABLE

France

Status: Immigration-status split not clean.

Official sexual-violence and crime datasets exist through SSMSI, Interior Ministry, Justice Ministry, and courts. Comparable migrant-status split needs exact table-level verification.

Available: police recorded sexual violence; justice/court lanes; victimization survey lanes.

Missing: irregular-status split; asylum-status split; conviction-by-immigration-status table.

NATIONALITY SPLIT PARTIAL

Germany

Status: Stronger source lane, but suspect data is not conviction.

Official BKA PKS sexual-offence data exists and German / non-German suspect categories are stronger than most countries, but non-German suspect does not mean illegal migrant.

Available: sexual offence totals; suspect data; German / non-German suspect categories; some immigration-context categories in official reporting.

Missing: clean conviction-by-irregular-status table; clean asylum-status-to-conviction bridge; charge-to-conviction bridge by legal status.

Official sexual-offence data and suspect-nationality lanes exist through BMI crime statistics, courts, and statistics office. Foreign suspect categories must not be read as illegal-entry categories.

OFFICIAL SEXUAL-OFFENCE STATS AVAILABLE

United States

Status: Immigration-status split not clean; federal/state data fragmented.

Official rape and sexual-assault data exists through FBI NIBRS, BJS, DHS, and state courts. There is no single clean national immigration-status conviction table across all jurisdictions.

Available: FBI offence lanes; BJS victimization/justice lanes; state court lanes; DHS immigration lanes.

Missing: single national immigration-status split; asylum-status split; visa-overstay split; state-to-federal conviction bridge.

Figures & Sources

Migration / crime figures

Checked: 1 July 2026

Numbers on this page are public-record leads. Crime, migration, sexual-offence, payout, death, and crisis figures must be tied to named official, court, police, parliamentary, regulator, or reputable source records before they are presented as settled.

Evidence standard

What counts as a usable figure?

Official statistics, court records, ministry releases, police datasets, parliamentary material, regulator data, or clearly named public reports. Nationality, foreign-born status, asylum status, immigration status, charge, suspect, conviction, and victim categories must not be mixed.

Document trail

Open the underlying record

When the figure matters, follow the document link, source file, court record, official release, PDF, book, or video. The visible page should help readers reach the underlying evidence fast.
