# Black File Launch Campaign

A starter campaign that routes readers through the Black File, trust rules, evidence vault, share kit, feed, download pack, and reader paths.

## Boundary

Campaign calendars are reusable public planning assets. They are not promises of automated or live posting.

## Core Routes

- Primary: black-file.html
- Share kit: share-kit-black-file-starter.html
- Download pack: dossier-pack-black-file-starter.html
- Feed: feed-main-signal.html
- Trust: trust-center.html
- Evidence: evidence-vault.html
- Reader path: sales-ladder.html

## 7-Day Campaign

- Day 1: introduce the Black File and the evidence-boundary rule.
- Day 2: share the Starter Pack and route readers to trust-center.html.
- Day 3: post a short hook from the Black File Starter Share Kit.
- Day 4: route readers to the Evidence Vault and Schema Index.
- Day 5: push the reader path and book universe.
- Day 6: recap the strongest archive doors.
- Day 7: invite readers to follow the Feed Center and Signal Board.
