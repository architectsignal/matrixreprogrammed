Boundary

Wealth estimates are estimates.

Forbes and Bloomberg rankings are useful signals, but they are not bank balances. Billionaire Watch tracks ownership, influence routes, public filings, donations, foundations and policy gravity.

Evidence Policy

Core Tracking Fields

Each billionaire profile should be structured the same way so daily updates become searchable intelligence.

Identity & Wealth Source

Name, citizenship, primary wealth source, company stakes, family office, trusts, public/private holdings and wealth-estimate source.

Daily Movement

Net-worth movement, market driver, company stock movement, private valuation change, asset sale, merger, IPO or court/regulatory change.

Control Routes

Voting shares, board control, founder rights, media ownership, cloud infrastructure, AI compute, satellites, pharma, defence, banking or housing exposure.

Political Gravity

Donations, PACs, lobbying, meetings, public endorsements, adviser roles, party funding, campaign infrastructure and policy lobbying.

Foundation / NGO Power

Foundations, grants, universities, health initiatives, digital ID, education, climate, disinformation/censorship projects and public-private partnerships.

Risk / Controversy

Court cases, regulator actions, sanctions, labor disputes, public criticism, tax disputes, monopoly pressure and national-security concerns.

What Gets Daily Updates?

Wealth Shock

Large net-worth move, company stock jump/drop, IPO, stake sale, dividend, valuation change or forced sale.

Political Money

Campaign donation, PAC funding, lobbying spend, adviser role, policy push, party meeting, think-tank donation or election infrastructure.

Public Contract

Company/foundation linked to state contract, defence deal, health-data system, cloud deal, AI system, space/satellite award or outsourcing route.

Foundation Move

Grant, university funding, global health initiative, education project, climate program, digital ID, media literacy or disinformation/censorship funding.

Media / Narrative

Media acquisition, platform policy change, content moderation shift, AI model deployment, news influence or public speech that moves markets/policy.

Legal / Regulator

Court case, antitrust action, tax dispute, sanctions exposure, insider trading claim, labor action, safety inquiry or regulatory settlement.
