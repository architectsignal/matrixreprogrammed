Boundary

Wealth estimates are estimates.

Forbes and Bloomberg rankings are useful signals, but they are not bank balances. Billionaire Watch tracks ownership, influence routes, public filings, donations, foundations and policy gravity.

Evidence Policy

Complete Person Dossier Fields

Each billionaire profile should be structured the same way so daily updates become searchable intelligence.

Identity & Wealth Source

Name, citizenship, primary wealth source, company stakes, family office, trusts, public/private holdings and wealth-estimate source.

Daily Movement

Net-worth movement, market driver, company stock movement, private valuation change, asset sale, merger, IPO or court/regulatory change.

Control Routes

Voting shares, board control, founder rights, media ownership, cloud infrastructure, AI compute, satellites, pharma, defence, banking or housing exposure.

Political Gravity

Donations, PACs, lobbying, meetings, public endorsements, adviser roles, party funding, campaign infrastructure and policy lobbying.

Foundation / NGO Power

Foundations, grants, universities, health initiatives, digital ID, education, climate, disinformation/censorship projects and public-private partnerships.

Risk / Controversy

Court cases, regulator actions, sanctions, labor disputes, public criticism, tax disputes, monopoly pressure and national-security concerns.

Complete Business Link Fields

Each billionaire is tracked through the companies, funds, foundations and networks around them.

Public Company Links

Public companies controlled, owned, chaired, founded or materially influenced; market cap; voting control; major government exposure.

Private Company Links

Private firms, family offices, venture funds, shell/holding entities where public, private valuations, major investors and acquisition history.

Foundation Links

Foundation assets, grants, recipient NGOs, universities, health programs, media projects, education initiatives and policy campaigns.

State Money Links

Contracts, subsidies, tax credits, loans, bailouts, defence awards, cloud procurement, space awards, health programs and public-private partnerships.

Political Links

Donations, PACs, lobbying, meetings, adviser roles, think tanks, candidate support, regulatory campaigns and influence networks.

Media / Narrative Links

Platform ownership, newspaper/media holdings, social-media policy, AI model distribution, news influence and censorship/disinformation funding.

Political Money

Campaign donation, PAC funding, lobbying spend, adviser role, policy push, party meeting, think-tank donation or election infrastructure.

Public Contract

Company/foundation linked to state contract, defence deal, health-data system, cloud deal, AI system, space/satellite award or outsourcing route.

Foundation Move

Grant, university funding, global health initiative, education project, climate program, digital ID, media literacy or disinformation/censorship funding.

Media / Narrative

Media acquisition, platform policy change, content moderation shift, AI model deployment, news influence or public speech that moves markets/policy.

Legal / Regulator

Court case, antitrust action, tax dispute, sanctions exposure, insider trading claim, labor action, safety inquiry or regulatory settlement.

Influence Scorecards

A billionaire entry is strongest when it scores the difference between wealth, control and policy influence.

Wealth Movement Score

0 none; 1 small daily move; 2 material weekly move; 3 major market shock; 4 company-changing event; 5 historic wealth transfer or control event.

Control Score

0 passive wealth; 1 minority stake; 2 board seat; 3 founder/control rights; 4 platform/infrastructure control; 5 public-policy or state dependency control.

State Exposure Score

0 no visible state route; 1 subsidies; 2 regulatory dependency; 3 public contracts; 4 defence/health/cloud/space contracts; 5 critical state infrastructure.

Narrative Power Score

0 none; 1 public speech; 2 social reach; 3 media/platform stake; 4 platform governance; 5 global information infrastructure.
