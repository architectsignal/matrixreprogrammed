# Matrix Brain

Expose wrongdoing. Help humanity. Follow the documents. Map the system.

## Boundary
The core tracks public records, policy moves, mandates, pilots, contracts, court records, official releases, corporate filings, source-linked reporting, and clearly labelled speculation. It does not treat association, rumour, symbolism, or internet lore as proof.

## Maintenance Rule
Every automated maintenance run must be visible through a workflow log, generated report, artifact, commit message, issue, or public change log.

## Signal Classes
- Public record: Court, law, official release, hearing, filing, contract, budget, treaty, or documented source item.
- Reported record: Dated reporting that points to documents, named sources, or official statements.
- Pattern: Multiple source-linked events showing a repeated institutional direction.
- Convergence: Two or more systems connect, such as identity plus money, health, speech, travel, policing, AI, or energy.
- Hypothesis: A structured question built from records, not a verdict.
- Speculation: Motif, internet claim, symbolic reading, or open-source lead requiring stronger evidence.
- Debunk lane: Claim-history and counter-source tracking for claims with weak, false, or unsupported source chains.
