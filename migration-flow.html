Evidence Rule

A border encounter, asylum application, removal, return, visa-overstay estimate, unauthorized-population estimate, and illegal-entry estimate are different categories.

Do not infer immigration status from nationality. Do not treat police suspects as convictions. Do not turn individual cases into group guilt.
