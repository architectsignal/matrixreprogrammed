Evidence Rule

No unsupported accusations. No private victim names. No screenshots as proof. No rumor framed as fact. Every item must either link to a source or be marked clearly as not verified.

CONFIRMED = official / court / verified source DEVELOPING = credible reporting, still moving ESTIMATE = modelled or NGO estimate REPORTED = claim logged, not necessarily verified COMPENSATED = claim accepted by scheme or court NOT VERIFIED = claim seen, not supported CAUSATION WARNING = report does not prove cause

Vaccines · Deaths, Damages & Evidence Boundary

This panel separates three different things that are often mixed together: adverse-event reports, compensated injuries/deaths, and proven causation. A report after vaccination is not automatically a death caused by vaccination.

VAERSSignal system only. VAERS accepts reports after vaccination; raw reports alone cannot prove causation.
U.S. VICPNo-fault compensation scheme for covered vaccines. Public reporting shows roughly $5B paid since 1988; exact current totals should be checked from HRSA reports.
COVID CICPCOVID countermeasure claims are handled separately from VICP. Use HRSA CICP reports for compensated claims and pending totals.
UK VDPSUK Vaccine Damage Payment Scheme uses a high disability threshold. The UK Covid Inquiry criticized support as inadequate and recommended reform.

Display Rule

Never write: “VAERS deaths = vaccine deaths.”

Use instead: “Deaths reported after vaccination,” “compensated death claims,” “confirmed causal finding,” or “claim pending,” depending on the source.
