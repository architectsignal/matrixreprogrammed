## Cross-Reference Rules
- Each timeline item must include a date, evidence class, people/entities, source door, what the record supports, and open questions.
- Names in timeline entries are record nodes, not automatic guilt labels.
- Repeated names across timeline, email, people, file cockpit, and network architecture sections become stronger network signals.
- Court findings and convictions outrank reporting, email trails, contact records, and speculation.
- Open questions must state what evidence would confirm, narrow, or falsify the theory.
