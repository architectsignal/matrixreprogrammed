What was Project Paperclip?

Project Paperclip was the post-World War II U.S. program that brought German scientists, engineers, and technical specialists into American projects. Matrix Reprogrammed treats it as a public-record origin lane for aerospace, weapons, intelligence, and Cold War science questions.
