## Boundary
Speculation-only layer. These subjects are tracked as claim-history, internet motifs, public-record-adjacent questions, debunk lanes, and open-source leads. They are not presented as fact. The page does not host illegal material, identify private victims, publish doxxing, describe graphic material, or make direct accusations against real people without source-specific evidence.

## Source Method
- Separate record, allegation, rumour, symbol, edited media, online lore, and court finding.
- Every subject needs: origin, core claim, evidence status, counter-source, what would change the classification, and safe next route.
- High-impact claims require high-grade evidence: court record, law-enforcement record, authenticated archive, forensic chain, official document, or credible named-source reporting.
- If a claim points toward illegal or exploitative material, the page tracks only the public claim-history and source debate, not the material itself.
