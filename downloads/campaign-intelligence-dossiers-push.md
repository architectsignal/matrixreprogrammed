# Intelligence Dossiers Campaign

A campaign for CIA, NSA, declassified archives, surveillance, MKUltra, intelligence maps, and agency dossier reader paths.

## Boundary

Campaign calendars are reusable public planning assets. They are not promises of automated or live posting.

- Primary: map-intelligence-network.html
- Share kit: share-kit-intelligence-network.html

## 7-Day Campaign

- Day 1: open with declassified archives and source-methodology boundaries.
- Day 2: route readers to the intelligence network map.
- Day 3: share the MKUltra / deep-state answer pages with caution labels.
- Day 4: push CIA and NSA book pages.
- Day 5: release the intelligence dossier pack.
- Day 6: post a short video caption from the share kit.
- Day 7: recap the agency archive and reader path.
