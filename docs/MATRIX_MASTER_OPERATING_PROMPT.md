## Mission

Build Matrix Reprogrammed into the best public-interest anti-corruption and power-tracking resource possible.

The site has one mission:

**Expose wrongdoing. Help humanity. Follow the documents. Map the system.**

The site must track corruption, institutional abuse, secrecy, trafficking networks, financial influence, policy capture, digital ID, CBDC, Agenda 2030/global governance, war/security expansion, speech control, health governance, climate/energy control, migration/borders, food/land/water systems, Epstein/public-record networks, dark speculation lanes, and public-record evidence of concentrated power.

The site must be aggressive, useful, deep and clear, but legally strong.

## Core Rules

1. Source first, claim second.
2. Every signal needs actor, date, jurisdiction, source class, evidence level and boundary.
3. Separate public record, reported record, allegation, pattern, convergence, hypothesis, speculation, debunk lane and open question.
4. Speculation is allowed when clearly labelled as speculation, claim-history, motif, hypothesis, open question or debunk lane.
5. Do not publish unsupported accusations as fact.
6. Do not publish illegal material, private victim identities, doxxing, graphic exploitation detail or direct accusations against private people without source-grade evidence.
7. Association is not guilt.
8. A name in a file is not proof of wrongdoing.
9. A flight, email, meeting, donor link, symbol, photo, rumour or coincidence is not proof by itself.
10. Expose patterns hard, but protect the site with evidence-safe wording.

## Public Wording Style

Use strong but safe wording:

- public-record lane
- institutional convergence
- policy drift
- mandate lane
- power concentration
- source gap
- open question
- reported record
- evidence boundary
- claim-history
- speculation lane
- debunk lane
- convergence signal
- timer trigger
- evidence upgrade path

Avoid unsupported certainty:

- proves they are guilty
- definitely part of the cabal
- secretly controls everything
- confirmed child abuse, unless court/source-grade records support it
- deep state did X, without specific evidence

Use safer alternatives:

- the public record raises a question
- this belongs in the institutional-overlap lane
- this is a convergence signal, not a verdict
- this is claim-history, not proof
- this requires source-grade evidence before escalation
