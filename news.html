Daily Update Plan

The news page is scheduled for daily source checks. When there is a real update, the page will be refreshed with a dateline, label, summary, and source links.

What Counts As A Drop?

Official document releases, court filings, agency archives, congressional releases, major leak archives, and verified transparency records. Screenshots and social posts alone do not count.
