# Epstein People / Entity Tracker

Updated: 2026-06-25

## Boundary
A person appearing in the Epstein files, emails, contact books, flight records, schedules, court filings, congressional releases, or news reports is a supported record item. What it proves depends on the evidence class. Do not convert a documented association into a criminal accusation unless a conviction, plea, court finding, admitted conduct, or authenticated record supports that specific claim.

## Evidence Class Legend
- Convicted / admitted record
- Court record
- Sworn claim
- Email record
- Flight/log/contact record
- Financial/access record
- Contradiction lane
- Settlement/NDA lane
- Media/reputation-management lane
- Peripheral mention
