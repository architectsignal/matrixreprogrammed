Official Boundary

The official record treats Jeffrey Epstein as deceased as of August 10, 2019. This watch does not claim he is alive. It tracks internet sightings, lookalike claims, fake-media claims, memes, and public speculation as claims only.

Hard rule: Do not harass, dox, name, or accuse ordinary lookalikes. A viral photo is not proof.
