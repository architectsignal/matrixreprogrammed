Boundary

Track power. Do not overclaim.

Complete Business Dossier Fields

Every company record in this lane should answer all of these before being treated as complete.

Corporate Identity

Ticker, exchange, HQ, subsidiaries, founding history, board, officers, founders, related ventures, major investors and controlling shareholders.

Products / Systems

Gotham, Foundry, AIP, Maven-related systems, deployment type, cloud dependency, data classes touched, user agencies and operational role.

Contract Map

Agency, contract number, ceiling, obligated value, start/end dates, procurement vehicle, option years, prime/subcontractor role and protest history.

Customer Dependency

How hard it is for the agency to leave: data migration, workflow dependence, source-code control, training dependence and renewal pressure.

Political / Lobbying Layer

Lobbying spend, policy asks, procurement language, public officials, revolving-door staff, think-tank allies and government advisory roles.

Controversy / Litigation

FOI fights, privacy claims, patient-data disputes, civil-liberties criticism, procurement challenges, parliamentary hearings and company responses.

Daily Trigger Matrix

Contract Trigger

New award, ceiling increase, option year, sole-source notice, renewal, subcontractor reveal, procurement protest or contract cancellation.

Data Trigger

Health, immigration, tax, defence, agriculture, police, benefits, supply chain, satellites or intelligence data enters a Palantir-linked platform.

People Trigger

New executive, board member, adviser, government official, lobbyist, investor, partner or former agency staff connection appears.

Policy Trigger

New AI, defence, procurement, health-data, immigration, cybersecurity or surveillance policy affects Palantir or its customers.

Financial Trigger

Earnings call, revenue split, stock shock, insider sale, analyst note, customer count, margin change or forecast revision.

Resistance Trigger

Lawsuit, FOI request, union complaint, patient opt-out issue, civil-liberties warning, parliamentary hearing or watchdog report.

Scorecards

Each daily record should receive practical scoring so readers know why it matters.

Money Score

0 none visible; 1 under $10m; 2 $10m-$100m; 3 $100m-$1B; 4 over $1B; 5 system-shaping multi-year ceiling.

Data Sensitivity Score

0 public data; 1 business data; 2 citizen services; 3 health/police/border; 4 defence/intelligence; 5 targeting or life-impacting systems.

Dependency Score

0 optional tool; 1 pilot; 2 workflow helper; 3 department platform; 4 enterprise system; 5 no easy exit / mission-critical.

Evidence Score

A official contract/filing; B court/regulator/parliament; C credible reporting; D commentary; X unsupported/rejected.
