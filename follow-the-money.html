Daily Update Format

Every daily update should answer the same questions so the page becomes a database, not just news.

1. What changed?

New contract, amended contract, award ceiling, court filing, lobbying disclosure, board appointment, donation, foundation grant, ownership change, investigation or public controversy.

2. Who benefits?

Company, agency, person, fund, foundation, prime contractor, subcontractor, adviser, lobbyist, donor, family office or political committee.

3. What is the money route?

Amount, ceiling, duration, payer, recipient, procurement vehicle, ownership stake, investment vehicle, grant route or tax/contract advantage.

4. What is the power route?

Data access, military capability, law enforcement use, health data, cloud infrastructure, border system, media influence, regulatory influence or political leverage.

5. What is the source grade?

A official filing/contract; B court/regulator/parliament; C credible reporting; D commentary; X unsupported or rejected.

6. Where does it route?

Live Intel, Evidence Vault, Power Atlas, Network Maps, Black File, book page, Rumble angle or downloadable briefing pack.
