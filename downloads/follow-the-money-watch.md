## Daily update rule

A daily update should be added when there is a new contract, procurement change, wealth shock, political donation, lobbying record, foundation grant, court/regulator action, board appointment, executive move, public-private partnership, AI/surveillance deployment, or major source correction.

## Evidence rule

No update should appear without an evidence label:

- A — official contract, filing, award, annual report or regulator release.
- B — court, parliament, sworn record, sanctions notice, lobbying/campaign-finance record.
- C — credible journalism or specialist trade reporting.
- D — commentary or analysis.
- X — unsupported or rejected.

## Reader boundary

This is a source-tracking and money-route system. It does not claim guilt, conspiracy, criminal conduct or secret control unless a high-grade source supports that claim.
